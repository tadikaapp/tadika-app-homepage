Features

Tadika App Main Features

Tadika App is designed to fullfill the everyday operations of a school or a daycare. The process begins when the kids step into the school, meeting with their friends and teachers, learn new things and play together, and conclude with the arrival of their parents or guardians. Parents or guardians are able to access Tadika App whenever and wherever they want, and keep track of their children's health and progress.

Teacher can benefit with Tadika App by logging the children and students activities and also manage the school or daycare. As the application is hosted on the cloud, Tadika App is accessible 24/7. Leverage the cloud technology and we ensured that your data and information are kept safe.



Record Your Student Activities and Progress seamlessly.

The software is available for teachers and parents 24 hours everyday. As soon as the student walk in to the school, teachers can record the attendance and other important information.

Demo session

We will help the school with the implementation of Tadika App in the school daily operations. The team is available to answer and guide the teachers and parents with any of their questions.

Easy to use

There is no installation required to start using Tadika App. Any computer with an internet browser and internet connection can access to the application.

Documentation

The documentation provides step by step on how to use Tadika App. We keep the documentation online and it is accessible to parents and teachers whenever and wherever they are. This documents are updated regularly.

About

Tadika App is an application that help school, daycare and kindergarten to manage their daily operations and strive to provide both parents and teachers on the best experience while allowing the children to learn and to be themselves at the school, while the teachers and parents work together to facilitate the children growth.

Tadika App is built in Malaysia, and we are a fully bootstrap startup company based in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia .
